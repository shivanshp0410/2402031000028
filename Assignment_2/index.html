📋 Day-by-Day Itinerary

6 days of unforgettable experiences

Day 1
Arrival in Dubai

Arrive at Dubai International Airport. Transfer to hotel, check-in and freshen up. Evening walk at Dubai Marina. Welcome dinner at a waterfront restaurant.

Day 2
Burj Khalifa & Dubai Mall

Visit the iconic Burj Khalifa (124th floor), explore Dubai Mall, watch the Dubai Fountain Show. Evening at leisure with skyline dinner.

Day 3
Desert Safari Adventure

Thrilling 4x4 dune bashing. Sunset camel ride. Traditional Bedouin camp dinner with live entertainment, belly dancing, and stargazing.

Day 4
Palm Jumeirah & Atlantis

Explore the Palm Jumeirah monorail. Visit Atlantis The Palm. Evening Dhow Cruise dinner on Dubai Creek.

Day 5
Old Dubai & Gold Souk

Visit Gold Souk, Spice Souk, and Al Fahidi Historic District. Abra boat ride. Farewell dinner at a luxury restaurant.

Day 6
Departure

Breakfast at hotel. Last-minute shopping at Dubai Duty Free. Transfer to airport for departure. Fond memories to last a lifetime!
